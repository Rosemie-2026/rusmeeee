Categories of Kapampangan Food

Main Dishes

Kapampangan main dishes are usually rich, hearty, and full of flavor. They often feature pork, beef, or chicken cooked with vinegar, soy sauce, or peanut sauce.

Kakanin (Native Sweets and Rice Cakes)

Kapampangan kakanin are sweet desserts made from glutinous rice and coconut milk. Examples include Tibok-Tibok, Bringhe, and Kalamay Hati.

Soups and Stews

Popular soups include Sinigang (sour tamarind soup) and Bulalo (bone marrow soup). These are comfort foods eaten regularly in Kapampangan homes.

Street Food and Snacks

Kapampangan street food includes Tocino, Tapa, and various grilled meats. These are affordable and widely eaten for breakfast or merienda.
