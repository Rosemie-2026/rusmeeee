Welcome to Lutong Kapampangan

Kapampangan cuisine is one of the richest and most celebrated food cultures in the Philippines. Known for bold flavors, unique ingredients, and centuries-old cooking traditions, the people of Pampanga have always taken great pride in their food.

However, as time passes and modern food takes over, many traditional Kapampangan dishes are being forgotten. This website aims to document and preserve those recipes before they are lost forever.

Did You Know?

"Pampanga is officially recognized as the Culinary Capital of the Philippines. Its people are known to be among the best cooks in the country."

Kapampangan cooking is special because it combines indigenous techniques with Spanish, Chinese, and American influences. The result is a unique cuisine that cannot be found anywhere else in the world.
