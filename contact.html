Contact Us / Share a Recipe

Do you know a traditional Kapampangan dish that is not on this website? Do you have a family recipe for one of the forgotten dishes? We want to hear from you. Every recipe shared helps preserve Kapampangan food heritage for future generations.

Why Share? Recipes shared with us may be added to this website (with your permission and credit). Your family's recipe could help ensure that a dish is never completely forgotten.
